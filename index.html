Introduction

Atlanta Braves is the official baseball team authorized by MLB, located in Atlanta, GA. The team has its young color spirit, which Atlanta Braves has great depth of players from farm systems.

For the batters, franchise player representing the team is first baseman, Freddie Freeman. Pitchers, a new rookie named Mike Soroka is leading the stats record as a starting pitcher.
